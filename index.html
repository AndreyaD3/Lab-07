All About Holidays / Festivals




Christmas is a holiday that is celebrated
during the end of the month of december.
It is celebrated with family and celebrates
the day Jesus was born. It is a time to
spend with family.Most families put christmas
decorations and trees around their homes



Halloween is a holiday celebrated on october 31st to
honour dead and ward off evil spirits.
People celebrating halloween dress up in costume and it
is tradition for children to go "trick or treating",
receiving candy as a treat.



New Years is a holiday celebrated on the first of January.
It marks the start of a new year. Families get together to
celebrate the new year together and count down the seconds
until midnight. People often make new years resolutions- changes
they want to see in themselves in the coming year.
